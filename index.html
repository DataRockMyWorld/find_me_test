What is the Find-Me app about?

Find-me app replaces the traditional methods of finding lost items.

It provides a dedicated platform to:

  • A dedicated platform to simplify reporting and searching for lost and found items.
  • Enhanced visibility of these items across a broader community.
  • Secure mechanisms to connect finders with owners and verify ownership
